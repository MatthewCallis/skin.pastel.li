Skin Editor lets you design & create your own Ableton Live skins.

How To Do A Thing

Click on a color to change it. Use the slider to change the opacity. Then click save at the bottom! Once saved, download the skin and move it to your Skins folder: C:/Program Data/Ableton Live 9 Suite/Resources/Skins or C:/Program Files/Ableton Live/Resources/Skins or /Applications/Ableton Live 9 Suite.app/Contents/App-Resources/Skins.
